About

I solve users' problems in creative ways, because I think you can have fun while making a positive change in someone’s life. I am currently studying Advertising at the University of Oklahoma and plan to attend graduate school to study Human-Computer Interaction after graduation.

My design philosophy is as follows: "I like to think of a digital product as a complex system: the big picture only works if the individual parts are healthy. I try to design by looking at the whole ecosystem, then zooming in on the specific pain points to fix the friction. Because the details are what make the entire experience run smoothly."

I am currently searching for Product Management, UX Design, and Product Marketing internships for Summer 2027.
