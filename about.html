About

I solve problems in creative ways, as I believe I can make a positive change in someone's life by using both the left and right sides of my brain. I am currently a senior studying Advertising at the University of Oklahoma, set to graduate in December 2026.

My core philosophy is: "I like to think of a product as a complex system: the big picture only works if the individual parts are healthy. I make decisions by looking at the whole ecosystem, then zooming in on the specific pain points to fix the friction. Because the details are what make the entire experience run smoothly."

I am currently open to internship opportunities aligning with Product Management, UX Design, and UX Research.
